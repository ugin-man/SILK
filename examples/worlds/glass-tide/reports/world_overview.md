# Glass Tide

Glass Tide is a compact maritime world about skilled life beside a tide that remembers yesterday's heat. At dawn the water sheds a fragile glass skin. The material is useful and beautiful, but it forgets within one tidal cycle, cracks in bulk, and cannot become an unlimited power source.

The world is anchored by the [Ember Tide](../subjects/pending/tidal_phenomena/ember-tide.md) and [First Light Terraces](../subjects/pending/settlements/first-light-terraces.md). Their connection reaches labor, street access, injury, storage, and debt. It does not explain everything. The [Pale Current](../subjects/pending/routes/pale-current.md) comes from meltwater and wind. [Cooling Houses](../subjects/pending/civic_practices/cooling-houses.md) derive authority from a local fire compact. The [Rain Vault](../subjects/pending/inland_archives/rain-vault.md) records rainfall inland and remains intentionally outside the coastal graph.

Readers can enter through the [catalog](../views/catalog.md), inspect [relations](../views/relation_map.md), or review disputed [claims](../views/claims.md). Every subject is generated, internally accepted, and still unreviewed by a human.
