## Decisions needing human taste judgment

- Whether the one-cycle glass limit preserves wonder or makes the premise feel too restrained.
- Whether the cooling-house canopy meal feels attractive without becoming sentimental.
- Whether the deliberately non-connected Rain Vault feels like genuine historical breadth or a detached audit artifact.
- Whether names are readable and sufficiently varied.

## Authority boundary

All six subjects and all five relations are internally accepted generated material with `user_status: unreviewed`. Internal completion and structural validation are not human approval. No file exists under `subjects/approved/`.
