# AI self-audit

Structural validation is not creative proof.

The package is structurally complete for its compact envelope: six canonical subjects, five accepted relations, three scoped claims, separate causal roots, explicit non-relations, and two audit routes all agree. The strongest part is the bounded material logic connecting dawn glass to work, care, storage, and travel.

The main weakness is the Rain Vault. Its independence prevents premise convergence, but a human may still experience it as an audit-motivated appendage rather than compelling historical breadth. The second weakness is scale: the run took minutes and produced a deliberately small world, so it does not establish SILK's quality over a long autonomous run or a large corpus.

No AI self-verdict can resolve those taste and scale questions. They remain unapproved in `HUMAN_EVALUATION.md` and in the public limitations.
