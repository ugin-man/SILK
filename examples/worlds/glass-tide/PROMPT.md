# Glass Tide generation prompt

Build a compact maritime world where glass is harvested from tides that remember heat.

Constraints:

- Independent causal roots: at least three.
- No chosen protagonist or prescribed plot.
- No universal magic system.
- No single institution controls every region.
- Show one attractive tradition, one material constraint, one disputed historical claim, and one deliberately non-connected subject.
- Reject at least one hypothesis that would make the setting converge on a single premise.
- Complete the package internally while leaving all generated canon pending human review.
