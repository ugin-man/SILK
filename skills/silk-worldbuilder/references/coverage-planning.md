# Adaptive coverage planning

## Purpose

Prevent a world from becoming a few deep files surrounded by names, without imposing universal categories or fixed counts.

## Derive obligations from the scope envelope

Build `coverage_map.yaml` from the world's promised reach, load-bearing systems, intended experience, and current maturity level. Coverage dimensions may include time, space, social position, material life, institutions, knowledge, ontology, ecology, language, or unknown world-specific axes.

Do not add a dimension because generic advice lists it. Add it because the world promise creates an obligation.

## Coverage cell

```yaml
- id: coverage.frontier.after_dragon_withdrawal
  dimensions:
    spatial: cinder_frontier
    temporal: post_withdrawal_present
    social: ordinary_households
    system: abandoned_dragon_defense
  importance: load_bearing
  represented_by:
    - world.subject.borrowed_watchtowers
  status: represented
  remaining_question: null
```

A cell is not automatically a new subject. Existing canonical files, relations, junctions, claims, or lived examples may represent it.

## Representative coverage

Choose cases that expose structural differences. A continent does not need one file per town. It may need a center, edge, route, barrier, internal exception, and outside perspective if those reveal how it works.

Do not sample every region with the same template. Derive representative and exceptional cases from their own causes.

## Coverage states

- `unexamined`
- `represented`
- `intentionally_absent`
- `deliberate_mystery`
- `not_relevant`
- `blocked`

Every non-represented state needs a reason. `Not relevant` is a valid decision.

`Not relevant` must explain why the scope envelope and load-bearing systems create no obligation. Bulk exclusions, repeated generic reasons, or exclusions that make the promised scale disappear are audit failures. Cold Reader Audit samples excluded cells and tries to show that at least one matters.

Do not shrink the scope envelope after generation merely to make existing coverage pass. Legitimate scope revision records the original promise, reason, user impact, discarded obligations, and approval requirement, then reopens the world charter and completion review.

## Level behavior

- Level 0 maps the envelope and seed obligations.
- Level 1 maps load-bearing vertical directions and independent roots.
- Level 2 maps system depth and lived consequences.
- Level 3 maps cross-domain and cross-scale intersections.
- Level 4 maps reader entry from multiple perspectives and verifies no promised area is only a name.

## Breadth debt

Flag breadth debt when the overview promises absent material, a region or era exists only in a list, all examples come from one center, global claims rely on one local case, asserted consequences have no representation, or the same representative pattern is copied across cells.

Queue the highest-impact debt. Do not solve it by generating equal quantities everywhere.
