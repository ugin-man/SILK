# Worked decision example

This example demonstrates process shape. Do not copy its lore into generated worlds.

## Gap

```yaml
id: gap.northern_belonging
level: 1
need: Give one northern polity a strong sense of local belonging.
desired_scope: regional
intended_appeal:
  - rooted
  - solemn
constraints:
  - do not redesign the world's entire metaphysics
```

## Spin

```yaml
hypotheses:
  - id: h.name_deity
    idea: A deity makes names metaphysically powerful.
    likely_scope: ontological
  - id: h.hearth_witness
    idea: A local presence recognizes membership through shared household fire.
    likely_scope: regional
  - id: h.burial_membership
    idea: Community membership is completed by burial in a shared field.
    likely_scope: local_to_regional
  - id: h.no_deity
    idea: Belonging remains a legal and social practice without a supernatural subject.
    likely_scope: regional
```

## Challenge before canon

The name deity may be interesting, but it poorly fits the requested job. Real power in naming creates obligations for aliases, place names, contracts, inheritance, language, crime, magic, and identity across the world.

```yaml
hypothesis: h.name_deity
verdict: replace
reason:
  - world-law blast radius exceeds regional purpose
  - explanation debt would make naming dominate unrelated systems
salvage:
  - supernatural recognition of belonging
```

The critic does not add exceptions to make name power regional. It selects the hearth witness for development because its scope and appeal fit, while retaining burial membership as a possible cultural practice rather than a second deity.

## Deepen

The vertical packet asks what the witness is, how recognition occurs, whether shared fire creates or acknowledges membership, what it cannot recognize, how migration and divided households work, how the polity historically adopted the practice, and how ordinary people experience it.

It does not automatically add commandments, clergy, a divine realm, enemy deity, sacred weapon, or universal family law.

## Weave

Propose relations to the polity's legal membership and household practice. Reject a proposed relation to every fire-using culture because shared fire alone is not a causal connection.

## Later claim boundary

A northern funerary technique uses ice to preserve a trace interpreted as the dead person's presence.

```yaml
claim: claim.ice.preserves_trace
scope: northern_funerary_practice
not_established_for:
  - natural_glaciers
  - southern_polar_regions
  - ice deities
  - ice creatures
```

When the southern polar region is developed, derive it from its own geography and role. Consider inheritance, mutation, independence, contradiction, and absence. Do not copy soul meaning and do not mechanically invert it.

## Integrate

The change set creates one canonical hearth-witness subject, updates the provisional collection and claim registry, adds two reviewed relations, records one rejected likely relation, closes the original gap, opens lived-consequence work, and records the discarded name-deity hypothesis only because its scope failure is reusable design memory.

## Lesson

Generate broadly, judge role and scope before canon, deepen without a stock template, weave effects rather than keywords, and preserve boundaries against likely future convergence.
