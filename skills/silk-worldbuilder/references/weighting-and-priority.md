# Weighting and priority

## Subject weight

- `load_bearing`: changing or removing the subject changes the world's identity, operation, or promised scale.
- `structural`: connects or supports several load-bearing subjects and needs reliable internal logic.
- `supporting`: explains a meaningful consequence, variation, or local manifestation.
- `texture`: creates concrete lived presence without carrying broad dependencies.

Deliberate mystery is a knowledge state, not a lower weight. A mystery can be load-bearing and still require deep consequences, evidence, interpretations, and boundaries.

## Collection weight

Collections use separate navigation weight such as `primary`, `supporting`, or `edge`. A primary collection does not make every member load-bearing.

## Maturity expectation

- Load-bearing subjects normally reach subject Level 4 before internal completion.
- Structural subjects normally reach the level required by their dependents and completion evidence.
- Supporting subjects reach enough depth to answer their function without name-only gaps.
- Texture subjects may remain concise and should usually live inside another canonical page unless independently identifiable.

Do not inflate every subject to Level 4. Do not hide a load-bearing system behind a supporting label to complete early.

## Weight audit

Challenge declared weight against:

- the scope envelope and intended experience;
- how many load-bearing claims require the subject, without using raw link count alone;
- causal reach and reversibility cost;
- whether removing it changes the overview, world signature, or several mature subjects;
- whether the user would reasonably expect detailed treatment;
- whether coverage and completion evidence rely on it.

If a supposedly supporting subject carries these obligations, promote its weight and reopen maturity. If a supposed load-bearing subject can be removed without structural loss, deepen its real role, reweight, merge, replace, or discard it.

## Reweighting

Weight can change when a subject gains or loses dependencies, becomes a world signature element, is replaced, or proves unnecessary. Reweighting triggers maturity and coverage review.

## Priority is not weight alone

Select work using weight, blocked dependencies, contradiction severity, scope reach, rescue debt, user visibility, interest risk, and promotion value. A small relation cut can outrank a large load-bearing expansion when it prevents world-wide convergence.
