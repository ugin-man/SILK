# Domain router

Use specialized lenses only when the world creates a load-bearing obligation. These files are reasoning modules, not mandatory categories or page templates.

- Magic, technology, powers, and formal systems: `system-design-lens.md`
- History, eras, chronology, and historical memory: `history-and-chronology.md`
- Geography, routes, environment, creatures, and ecology: `geography-and-ecology.md`
- Polities, institutions, law, economy, family, and social organization: `society-and-institutions.md`
- Cultures, languages, identities, and transmission: `culture-and-language.md`
- Deities, cosmic structures, afterlives, nonhuman minds, and ontological difference: `cosmology-and-nonhuman.md`

Select the smallest useful set. A magical city may need systems, geography, and institutions. A timeless dream world may not need conventional chronology. A deity's canonical file may require cosmology and culture without becoming separate cosmology and culture documents.

When a world-specific domain is not covered, derive a new lens from the universal questions in `deepening-engine.md` rather than forcing it into the closest familiar category.
